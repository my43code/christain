2026 Admission Is Now Open

Philippine Christian University, in partnership with Waigani Christian College, which is in the process of becoming Papua New Guinea Christian Institute of Higher Education (PNGCIHE) is excited to announce that we are now accepting applications for 2026 academic year. This strategic collaboration ensures students receive quality, internationally aligned education while PNGCIHE progresses toward full institutional recognition in one of Papua New Guinea’s higher education sectors.
All academic programs currently offered under the partnership between Philippine Christian University (PCU) and Waigani Christian College (WCC) are undergoing formal accreditation with the Department of Higher Education, Research, Science and Technology (DHERST).
Admission is Now Open for past and present grade 12 students. Secure your place in a transformative learning journey. Contact us today to learn more about program offerings, entry requirements, and upcoming intakes.
Before You Apply! Please read the Eligibility Checklist before submitting your 2026 Application Form. Download the checklist and FAQs from our Facebook page or scan the QR Code below. Applications can be emailed to admission@pngcu.ac.pg, or hand delivered to the Tertiary Wing of Waigani Christian College.
