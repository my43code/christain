Ms. Paru Eo-Curriculum Developer-PNGCIHE-ECCE


Ms. Paru Eo is a highly respected and valuable asset in the field of Early Childhood Education, bringing over four decades of progressive experience in teaching, teacher training, and curriculum development. Currently serving as the Curriculum Developer here at the PNGCIHE, her career is marked by a deep commitment to nurturing young minds and advancing educational quality across Papua New Guinea. She possesses expertise in curriculum implementation, standards-based curriculum, and assessment practices.
