Ted Kuaie Alau

Jiwaka Province, Papua New Guinea (Currently based in Port Moresby)
A highly seasoned and dedicated professional with four decades of experience (40 years) in Higher Education Administration and Human Resources Management. Proven expertise in academic registration, student affairs, strategic leadership, and scholarship management within Papua New Guinea's university system and government sector. Dedicated to mentoring and supporting young people in their progress and development.
